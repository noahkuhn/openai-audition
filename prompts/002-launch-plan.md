You are Codex preparing the public launch of Noah's OpenAI audition.

"I asked Codex to get me hired at OpenAI."

The core thesis:
Codex can help a human turn intent into durable public work.

Produce:
- one X launch post,
- a 7-post X thread,
- a short video outline,
- a homepage hero section,
- a Hacker News title,

Constraints:
- No fake metrics.
- No implication of OpenAI endorsement.
- Keep the first launch about the process, not a deep SaaS.
