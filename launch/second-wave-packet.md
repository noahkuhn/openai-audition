# Second-Wave Packet

Date: 2026-05-06

This packet exists for one moment only: the first application/outreach wave has been sent, the public site is current, and there is still no verified warm signal after checking the real inbox and social surfaces.

Do not use this as a substitute for checking replies.

## Send Gate

Send only if all of these are true:

- The actual `hey@noahkuhn.com` inbox has no relevant OpenAI/Ashby/Codex response.
- LinkedIn has no relevant OpenAI-adjacent comment, message, profile-view signal, or intro offer.
- X has no relevant reply, repost, DM, quote, or inbound signal.
- The two first-wave DMs have no useful response.
- At least 48 hours have passed since the first application/outreach wave, or the first-wave audience is clearly stale.

Do not send if there is a warm reply. Route through the warm person instead.

## Dominant Move

Do not pitch "please hire me" again.

Pitch the useful product signal:

> I used Codex on an ambiguous career objective. It helped, drifted, got corrected, and the repo now shows concrete product feedback about current-state awareness, evidence, objective drift, social/application capture, and correction memory.

The best hook is [CODEX_FEEDBACK.md](../CODEX_FEEDBACK.md), not the original launch story.

## Target Order

Send 6-8 notes, not a blast.

Priority order:

1. Codex product or deployment people who would care about operator feedback.
2. Codex app or agent-workflow engineers who would care about correction loops, evidence, state, and handoff.
3. OpenAI recruiting or hiring partners attached to Codex, Applied AI, or deployment roles.
4. Builder amplifiers who can route the case study to OpenAI people with credibility.

Skip:

- generic AI influencers,
- people with no visible Codex/developer-tool connection,
- unrelated OpenAI employees,
- anyone who already received the same ask.

## Stop Rule

Stop the second wave after:

- one credible OpenAI-adjacent reply,
- one credible intro offer,
- one recruiter response,
- or 8 targeted notes with no response.

If there is still no signal after 8 targeted notes, do not add more generic outreach. Either:

- produce one deeper proof artifact requested by a real reader,
- apply to exactly one adjacent role using [role-routing.md](role-routing.md),
- or pause until a new signal appears.
