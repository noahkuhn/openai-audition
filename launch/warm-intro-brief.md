# Warm Intro Brief

Date: 2026-05-06

Purpose: make it easy for someone else to route this to the right OpenAI/Codex person without needing the full backstory.

Use this when a friend, colleague, investor, builder, recruiter, or OpenAI-adjacent contact is willing to forward the project.

Do not use this as a cold blast. Use [second-wave-packet.md](second-wave-packet.md) for cold or semi-warm second-wave notes.

Use [warm-routing-shortlist.md](warm-routing-shortlist.md) to choose the right Codex lane before asking someone to forward this.

## The Ask

```text
Could you forward this to the person at OpenAI who owns Codex deployment, product feedback, or agent-workflow quality?
```

The goal is not a favor for a job slot. The goal is to get the artifact in front of the person who would understand the signal.


## Current Timing Hook

OpenAI's 2026-04-16 `Codex for (almost) everything` release frames Codex as moving beyond coding into the broader software-development workflow: computer use, more connected tools and apps, memory, learning from previous actions, ongoing tasks, and proactive continuation from prior context.

That makes this audition timely. The repo is a live user-side case study of exactly those surfaces: Codex using files, connected tools, browser/computer use, memory, social/application evidence, and long-running follow-through on one high-stakes objective.

Reference:

- <https://openai.com/index/codex-for-almost-everything/>

## Boundaries

Do not claim:

- OpenAI endorsed the project.
- Codex acted without human supervision.
- The application has produced a response.
- The social posts, DMs, or application confirmation are publicly verified.

Do say:

- The first application/outreach wave is already done.
- The public artifact is current as of the latest repo state.
- The feedback memo is the best artifact for Codex product/deployment readers.
- The desired outcome is a serious OpenAI/Codex conversation.

## Stop Rule

If a forwarded note gets a warm reply, stop sending more intros and route through that person.

If three credible warm-intro asks get no response, do not keep asking the same circle. Use [second-wave-packet.md](second-wave-packet.md) for a broader but still targeted follow-up.
