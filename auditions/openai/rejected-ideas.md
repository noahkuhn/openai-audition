# Rejected Ideas

## Generic AI Wrapper

Rejected because:

- Too easy to dismiss.
- Does not show special taste.
- Does not connect strongly enough to Codex's product direction.

## Resume Generator

Rejected because:

- The premise is weaker than the campaign.
- The world does not need another cover letter tool.
- The point is proof of work, not better job documents.

## Crypto-First Project

Rejected as the primary angle because:

- It narrows the audience immediately.
- It shifts attention away from the meta-story.
- It makes the campaign about Noah's niche instead of Codex's leverage.

Kept as:

- possible proof-object material if it wins through the scorecard.

## Fake Autonomous Company

Rejected because:

- Too common.
- Too easy to exaggerate.
- Too likely to drift into theater without proof.

## Deep SaaS Before Narrative

Rejected because:

- Slows launch.
- Creates product complexity before attention exists.
- Risks burying the cleanest hook.
